# PRD: Minecraft Smore Mod

## 1. Introduction
*   **Mod Name:** Minecraft Smore
*   **Target Minecraft Version:** Java Edition 1.21+
*   **Overview:** A mod introducing new food items, crafting recipes, and villager trades centered around creating and consuming "Smores" for unique benefits.

## 2. New Items

### 2.1. Cinnamon
*   **Description:** A small, reddish-brown stick.
*   **Acquisition:** Traded from a Cleric villager (1 Emerald for 1 Cinnamon, 95% probability).
*   **Usage:** Crafting ingredient for Graham Crackers. No other uses.

### 2.2. Graham Cracker
*   **Description:** A light brown, square-shaped cracker.
*   **Acquisition:** Crafted.
*   **Usage:** Crafting ingredient for Raw Smore. Can be eaten to gain Regeneration 2 for 5 seconds.

### 2.3. Chocolate Bar
*   **Description:** A dark brown, rectangular bar divided into segments.
*   **Acquisition:** Crafted.
*   **Usage:** Crafting ingredient for Raw Smore. Can be eaten to gain Regeneration 2 for 5 seconds.

### 2.4. Marshmallow
*   **Description:** A soft, white, cylindrical confection.
*   **Acquisition:** Crafted.
*   **Usage:** Crafting ingredient for Raw Smore. Can be eaten to gain Regeneration 2 for 5 seconds.

### 2.5. Raw Smore
*   **Description:** An uncooked assembly of a graham cracker base, marshmallow, and chocolate, topped with another graham cracker.
*   **Acquisition:** Crafted.
*   **Usage:** Can be placed on a campfire to be cooked.

### 2.6. Cooked Smore
*   **Description:** A golden-brown, slightly melted smore, still in its cracker sandwich form.
*   **Acquisition:** Cooked from a Raw Smore on a campfire.
*   **Usage:** Edible. Consuming provides 15 extra hearts that persist until death. Can only be consumed once per life. Triggers brown particle effects (cracker crumbs) and a crunching sound upon consumption.

## 3. Recipes

### 3.1. Graham Cracker
*   **Ingredients:** 4 Wheat, 1 Cinnamon, 4 Sand
*   **Yield:** 2 Graham Crackers
*   **Shape:** Shapeless

### 3.2. Chocolate Bar
*   **Ingredients:** 4 Sugar, 1 Cocoa Bean
*   **Yield:** 2 Chocolate Bars
*   **Shape:** Shapeless

### 3.3. Marshmallow
*   **Ingredients:** 5 Sugar, 4 Dried Kelp
*   **Yield:** 4 Marshmallows
*   **Shape:** Shapeless

### 3.4. Raw Smore
*   **Ingredients:** 4 Marshmallows, 2 Graham Crackers, 2 Chocolate Bars, 1 Diamond
*   **Yield:** 1 Raw Smore
*   **Shape:** Shapeless

## 4. Mechanics

### 4.1. Villager Trades
*   **Cleric Villager:** Trades 1 Emerald for 1 Cinnamon with 95% probability.

### 4.2. Smore Cooking
*   A Raw Smore placed on a campfire will cook into a Cooked Smore in 3 seconds.
*   Once cooked, a Smore cannot be uncooked.
*   Upon cooking, the Smore pops off the fire (like other cooked food items).

### 4.3. Cooked Smore Effects
*   Grants 15 extra hearts (normal hearts) that persist until the player dies.
*   Can only be consumed once per player life.
*   Attempting to consume a second Cooked Smore in the same life will have no effect.
*   Upon consumption, brown particle effects (cracker crumbs) and a crunching sound are triggered.
